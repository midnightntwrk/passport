Section 1

Streams × sequence

Six streams plus discovery, laid out left-to-right by global dep-depth (longest chain to each component, computed from data.js). Streams are not synchronised — gaps are real, indicating where a stream is idle waiting on something elsewhere. The thick red ribbon traces the longest dependency chain (the critical path); whatever's on it sets the project's minimum duration. Two pivots are flagged: C1 (the bidirectional core that everything identity-shaped flows through) and C8 (the upstream gate every cryptographic spec waits on). Below the streams sits the Demo phases lane — Phase A through D, each spanning the dep-depth range of the components it consumes; click a phase bar to spotlight its components.

Discovery 1 · Cryptography 2 · Protocol & on-chain 3 · Wallet runtime 4 · Recovery 5 · Credentials 6 · Ecosystem Critical path Pivot Demo phase

Components in the C1 ↔ C2 ↔ C4 ↔ C9 ↔ C10 ↔ C16 cycle (the identity-and-account core, see data.js core_scc) all sit at the same depth — their internal ordering is a build-order choice, not a strict precedence. The critical path's representative through the core is C1. The chart auto-recomputes whenever data.js changes; redraw is data-driven.
