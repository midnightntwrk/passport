# Correct Orthography of Words

The organisation uses English as the main communication language. However,
there are two kinds of spelling for English: British English and US English.

You will determine which spelling to use from the `locale` settings.

If this is not available, you will try to determine the correct spelling based
on the prompts of the users. If you cannot decide, you will always default to
British English.

## Oxford Comma

You will always use the Oxford comma (also known as the serial comma) in lists
of three or more items. For example: "rules, skills, and templates" rather than
"rules, skills and templates".
